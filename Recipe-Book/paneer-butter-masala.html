Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 medium onions (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté till golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté till the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil starts separating.
  5. Add red chili powder, turmeric, salt, and garam masala. Mix well.
  6. Add paneer cubes and a splash of water. Simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  7. Add fresh cream and stir gently.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or roti.
