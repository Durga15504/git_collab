Lemon Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (cooled)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp urad dal
  • 1/2 tsp chana dal
  • 1-2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 dried red chili
  • 8–10 curry leaves
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp peanuts or cashews
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add urad dal, chana dal, peanuts, and sauté until golden.
  3. Add green chilies, red chili, curry leaves, and turmeric. Sauté for 30 seconds.
  4. Add cooked rice and salt. Mix well on low flame.
  5. Turn off the heat and add lemon juice. Mix gently.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves. Serve warm or pack for lunch!
