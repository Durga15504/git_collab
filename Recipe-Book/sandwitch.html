Veg Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bread
  • 1 boiled potato (sliced)
  • 1 small cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 small tomato (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp green chutney (mint & coriander)
  • Butter (as needed)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chaat masala (optional)

Instructions

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Spread green chutney over the buttered side.
  3. Layer sliced potato, cucumber, and tomato on one slice.
  4. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and chaat masala if using.
  5. Cover with another slice of bread (buttered side inside).
  6. Grill or toast the sandwich until golden and crisp.
  7. Slice diagonally and serve with ketchup or chutney.
